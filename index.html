The Game

OrBlock is a game that's all about dodging ever-moving walls. Sounds simple, right?

So, let us make things more interesting...
You only have one move per obstacle!
Too challenging? Try to get upgrades as you level up, and use jumping, bouncing of walls and extra lives to your advantage.
